Jamshedpur Integrated Supply & Services provideS custom services for our clients to reduce cycle time and increase overall output.

Wood Log detection

In the Iron ore mines of Noamundi, Jharkhand we had the challenge to reduce the cost of equipment damage caused by the rubble and wood logs which mixed with the iron ore and jammed the equipment. Our team built an in-house solution using Machine learning and AI algorithm, we trained a program using 1000 wood log images to independently detect any wood logs on the conveyor.

Coal Mine Wireless Radio Signal System

Mining is the beginning of any process in a Iron and steel company and it runs 24*7 all days a year. However its not a cheap process , the directorate general of mines keeps a check on power and energy consumption of mining facilities all over india, the challenge- Coal mines of west Bokaro needed to control and monitor the over usage of electricity . For this we proposed monitoring of various features of mines such as HT motor LT motor, pumps etc , Due to the ever changing terrain of the mines , we could not use a solution which uses cables and wiring, we had to implement LORA which referrers to 2.4 GHz long distance short wave radio waves and control units in all the power adapter points , We were able to achieve a output of all IO controls in a control room and due to the proper usage of features and no energy wastage , there was a reduction in electricity costs.

Pharmaceutical order management

Our client was a large scale medicine Stock holder and supplier and had the challenge to determine the appropriate stock carrying for market situation. We made a ML based application which used last 10 years data based on a research of 10lakh users , it tells us which form of medicine would be in more demand in which area and in which time of the year, this app can be used all over India, it uses geo metadata and local information and tells a pharma client which medicine to stock up on.

Dynamic Vehicle Control system

To monitor and enhance performance of vehicles we developed an app which can overwrite the manufacturer setting and tune the vehicle for the owners needs such as better fuel efficiency , softer/ stiffer suspension, temperature of air intake and this is as easy as plugging an OBD connector and switching the vehicle on and configuring the rest on your mobile device, expensive services are now a thing of the past, we believe in reducing costs, all the way.
