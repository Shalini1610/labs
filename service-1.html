Manpower & Maintainance

Ensuring Higher Efficiency

Jamshedpur Integrated Supply & Services proudly supports and runs a fleet of over 400 trained workmen (high skilled ,medium skilled and unskilled). We help our clients achieve their monthly targets by providing our skills and strategies through manpower supply . We believe strategy is the most important aspect of making any change. Here are a few of our past jobs.

Tube Organic Coating (TOC )

Organic coating improves life of the tube by eliminating the probability of rusting of tube in the long run and finds its application in a wide application of industry and household water supply. JISS was assigned the Process, Paint, End Cap, Packing and Stacking in bundles of the TOC line in May 2020, tackling the post COVID scene, first month was a challenge , we strategized and the results came in the next month, TOC line output jumped from 125 tons to 165 tons per month and we are expecting to achieve 220 tons per month by year end.

Furnace Operation

Job was assigned to JISS in Oct 2019 , 6 high skilled employees were deployed round the clock, we noticed various bottle necks in the operations and tackled them one by one with close consultation with the officers, the result- an operation which never output more than 4000 metric tons in the past gave the output of 4600 metric tons recording a growth of 15% in production just in 4 months of our service.

Equipment Upgradation

When we take up a manpower/maintenance supply job, we not only see the efficiency and output but we also give regular feedback to the higher end team officers as to how we can achieve less break downs and make the operation more efficient. We closely monitored the Normalizing furnace’s breakdowns and studied the failures , reviewed and strategized a maintenance plan which reaped a 12% reduction in shutdown time, more plant availability = more production. Same was applied to a cold-draw process plant which showed high shutdowns , we achieved 18% more plant availability through Planned maintenance.

Beveling

We have also experienced a requirement of beveled tubes , instead of outsourcing we developed an inhouse process to manufacture beveled tubes saving the company approx. 5 crore per year, we achieved 3000MT/ month of production in yearly average.

Testing Jobs

We perform a wide range of stress testing of products and equipment, In the past we tested a tube at 3000psi and certified it for a higher product value in the market, apart from this, we do pneumatic cylinder stress test, heat test, pressure test etc, in our product lab.
